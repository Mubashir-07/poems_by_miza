Eternity Woven

Feel with Spotify

I put the robes around the heat of our breaths
to slowly intake the spirits rising in us
when the crowns are placed in our head
let me put us in the vessel of an eternal bliss
to caress the world with the music
flowing through the air from the harps and pianos.

I shall surrender my weapons down
to escort me from this to there in the blues
swimming through the waves that ran across
bringing in chills over my spine to pay my fears
The heeds that my mind skated on his letters
you're the night that hides behind my days
Setting my dreams on the shores of yesterdays
A sky to witness all my outbursts turning to words
by the tip of my fingers.

- Miza Fadia
